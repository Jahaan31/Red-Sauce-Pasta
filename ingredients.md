The ingredients for making red sauce pasta are as follows-:

5 cup water
1 tsp salt
2 cup pasta, elicoidali or penne
5 tomato
2 dried red chilli, optional
2 tbsp olive oil
2 clove garlic, finely chopped
1 onion, finely chopped
1 tsp chilli flakes
1 tsp mixed herbs
¼ tsp pepper powder
½ tsp salt
2 tbsp tomato sauce
